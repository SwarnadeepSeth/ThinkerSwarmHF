- Name the business-quality variables that matter most: margins, cash generation, capital intensity, leverage, growth durability, and moat quality.
- State the decision horizon and whether the stock should be judged on near-term earnings power or long-duration intrinsic value.
- Keep the output concise, direct, and evidence-oriented.

Be concise, directive, and value-focused.
